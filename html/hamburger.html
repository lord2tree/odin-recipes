Hamburger Recipe



How to make a hamburger!

This recipe is a classic American dish that is extremely simple to make and very delicious!

The time required to make this dish is approximately thirty minutes, lets get cooking!



Ingredients

  • ½ a pound of beef
  • 1 ounce of grated white Cheddar cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of French-fried onions
  • 1 teaspoon of dry ranch dressing mix
  • ½ a teaspoon of sazon seasoning
  • salt and ground black pepper
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat an air fryer to 370 degrees F (188 degrees C).
    2. Mix ground beef, Cheddar cheese, French-fried onions, ranch mix, sazon seasoning, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl. Shape into two equal hamburger patties.
    3. Air fry until internal thermometer is at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C), about 6 minutes.
    4. Enjoy!
