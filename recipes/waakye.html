Ingredients

  • 2 cups of rice
  • 1 cup of black-eyed peas
  • 3 cups of water
  • 1 can of coconut milk
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger, grated
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of smoked paprika
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice and black-eyed peas in cold water and soak them separately for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium-high heat and add the onions, garlic, and ginger. Saut√© for a few minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the spices (coriander, cumin, smoked paprika, and cayenne pepper) and stir for a minute until the spices become fragrant.
  4. Drain the water from the black-eyed peas and add them to the pot along with the coconut milk and 3 cups of water. Bring to a boil.
  5. Add the soaked rice and bay leaf to the pot and stir well. Reduce the heat to low and cover the pot.
  6. Simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed. Make sure not to stir the rice too much as it can become sticky.
  7. Once the rice is cooked, remove the bay leaf and fluff the rice with a fork. Add salt to taste.
  8. Serve hot with your choice of garnishes, such as boiled eggs, fried plantains, avocado, or tomato and onion salsa. Enjoy the delicious and authentic taste of Waakye!
